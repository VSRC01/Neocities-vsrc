About.____________________________

I’m an architect in training and a developer by restlessness.

I work in the space between the physical and the digital, where projects take shape both in concrete and in code. I see architecture and programming as poetic languages. My practice blends technique, experimentation, and critique, always guided by the search for meaningful structures — whether built or written.

I have a particular interest in technology and everything that exists on the edge between function and expression. I like to understand how things work — and even more to dismantle and rebuild them.

Currently, I’m developing projects that move between spatial design and the computational universe — exploring everything from interactive visualizations to data-driven narratives.

This site is a fragment of it all: process, trace, and provocation.
